Preschool Program

Preschool children enjoy spending time with other youngsters their own age and becoming involved with group activities. Structured educational programs introduce them to basic concepts in language arts, math, science and social studies. We have specifically designed learning centers for individual and group projects that encourage children to make discoveries about themselves, fellow beings and the world we all share and live in.

Now offering a new Spanish program!* Introducing Sonrisas Spanish: our new, hands-on learning program aimed to help children set foot on the path to becoming bilingual and bi-literate. Our active and fun approach includes age-appropriate activities that support language acquisition through:

  • Music and verse
  • Games, role-play and drama
  • Calendar activities
  • Reading authentic literature
  • Art projects
  • Independent practice with Student Portfolio

Kindergarten Program

Our kindergarten program has been carefully designed to meet children's unique needs while preparing them for elementary school.

* Program highlights include:

  • Teachers who encourage children to reach developmental and academic milestones
  • Creative, hands-on, and project-based learning
  • Support for core pre-academic areas including literacy, mathematical reasoning, and scientific investigation
  • Multiple and diverse learning activities that ensure children thrive in all areas of development
  • Safety, security, and cleanliness practices that meet or exceed all state, local, and national guidelines
